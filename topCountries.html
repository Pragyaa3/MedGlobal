Study MBBS Abroad: Top Countries

Study MBBS in Russia

Russia is a popular destination for Indian students due to its high-quality education and affordable MBBS programs. Russian universities offer MBBS in English, and several institutions are recognized globally for their medical programs. The cost of studying in Russia is affordable, with tuition fees ranging from INR 2 to 8 Lakhs annually, and the duration of the MBBS program is 6 years, including a 1-year internship.

Program MBBS
Duration 6 years (5+1)
Intake Sept-Oct
Eligibility 50% in 12th board exam
NEET Mandatory
Cost INR 4 Lakhs/annum
Living Cost USD 400/month

Study MBBS in Uzbekistan

Uzbekistan is emerging as an attractive destination for medical aspirants due to affordable tuition fees and internationally recognized medical schools. The country offers MBBS programs in English, and students can complete their studies at a fraction of the cost compared to other countries. The course lasts for 6 years, and students can avail themselves of various scholarships to ease the financial burden.

Program MBBS
Duration 6 years
Intake Sept-Oct
Eligibility 50% in 12th board exam
NEET Mandatory
Cost INR 3 Lakhs/annum
Living Cost USD 300/month

Study MBBS in Kazakhstan

Kazakhstan offers a globally recognized MBBS program with an English-medium curriculum. Known for its medical excellence, Kazakhstan offers affordable fees, quality education, and an enriching cultural experience for Indian students. The course duration is 6 years, with several institutions providing the option to apply for scholarships.

Program MBBS
Duration 6 years
Intake Sept-Oct
Eligibility 50% in 12th board exam
NEET Mandatory
Cost INR 4 Lakhs/annum
Living Cost USD 350/month

Study MBBS in Philippines

The Philippines is known for its affordable MBBS programs and a strong medical curriculum that is recognized internationally. The medium of instruction is English, and the duration of the program is 5 years, followed by a 1-year internship. The cost of living is affordable, and the admission process is straightforward.

Program MBBS
Duration 5+1 years
Intake May-June
Eligibility 50% in 12th board exam
NEET Mandatory
Cost INR 3.5 Lakhs/annum
Living Cost USD 300/month

Study MBBS in Georgia

Georgia is quickly becoming a top choice for Indian students who wish to study MBBS abroad due to its high-quality education, low tuition fees, and globally recognized universities. The MBBS program is taught in English, and the course lasts 6 years, including a 1-year internship.

Program MBBS
Duration 6 years
Intake Sept-Oct
Eligibility 50% in 12th board exam
NEET Mandatory
Cost INR 3 Lakhs/annum
Living Cost USD 350/month
