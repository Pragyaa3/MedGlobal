Eligibility & Conditions for Indian Medical Aspirants

Eligibility Criteria

  • At least 50% marks in 12th board exam.
  • 50% in Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and English.
  • Age between 17 to 25 years (No upper age limit in Russia).
  • NEET mandatory for Indian medical students.
  • All legal and authentic documents required.

Documents Required

  • 10th and 12th Marksheets
  • School-leaving certificate
  • NEET Scorecard
  • Code of Conduct Certificate
  • Medical fitness certificate/report
  • Bank statements of parent/guardian
  • Passport (scanned copies)
  • Passport Size Photos
  • Police Clearance certificate
  • Marksheets of 10th and 12th grades
  • Application form required as per the university
  • In majority of the universities, English proficiency test certificate (TOEFL/IELTS) is not required

Admission Process for Indian Students

Step-by-step guide for Indian students applying for MBBS abroad.

  • Shortlist country and university.
  • Fill out the online form and upload documents.
  • Receive acceptance letter.
  • Pay tuition fee and receive invitation letter.
  • Start visa process.

Major Benefits & Perks

  • Affordable compared to Indian private medical universities.
  • No hefty donations required.
  • World-class education and infrastructure.
  • Low cost of living in countries like Russia and Georgia.
  • Exposure to international culture and opportunities.
